MeAndYouCrew is a fun, active, social clan. We like to have people of all ages in the clan, bringing a diverse amount of youth and age into a winning strategy (kids in this clan are held to the same standard of maturity as adults). Family and friends are always accepted because they add more fun to this game. We love to war all the time, but at the same time like to have players that focus on other aspects such as farming, and trophy pushing. We tend to get pretty serious about trying to win every war, but if we lose a war, we know that we tried everything we could to win.

Our clan was originally founded by Freyja, and then turned over to Bryan due to Freyja's retirement. Even with a change in leadership, the clan never missed a beat and still continues it's previous winning ways to this day. We pride ourselves on having a strong group of core members who are the heart and soul of the clan, and are actively growing and learning within the clan. Our leadership has also proven to be fair and just to all, with the clan's best interest in mind at all times.

In wars, we require that both attacks be used in every war. If players do not use both attacks then they are subject to being kicked from the clan. We understand if you do not want to participate in wars, or if real life gets in the way at times. In these cases, simply opting out of wars will draw no consequences in the clan, unless your base becomes inactive for a season.

Donations are a very big deal in this clan. We encourage everyone in the clan to actively donate. The most important when requesting in this clan is to think of how others are impacted by your requests. Expensive troops, such as valkyries, golems, dragons, etc, are not given lightly in this clan, and generally carry the requirement that they be used in war only. We require that everyone does not abuse other member's resources for their own gain through donations.

Elder in this clan is earned. General requirements to earn elder are to be active in the clan, be a skilled attacker, put the clan's well being before themselves, and have 3-6 months of experience within the clan. Co-Leader is seldom given out. Only people who perform exceptional services for the clan are considered for this position.
